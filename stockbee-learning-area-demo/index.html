2LYNCH
2
Not up 2 days in a row A small up day of up to 2% before b/o is fine. Depending on price of stock.
L
Linearity of prior move.
Y
Young trend 1 to 3rd b/o from consolidation is low risk. As trend ages risk of failure increases.
N
Narrow range day or negative day pre breakout
C
Consolidation/pullback is shallow, orderly and compact with narrow range bars and low volume. No more than one 4% b/d in consolidation.
H
Close price is near the high of the day
